I am a data scientist at Jellyfish and a Lecturer for the MS in Applied Data Science program at the University of Chicago. Prior to joining Jellyfish I was a visiting assistant professor in the anthropology department at the University of Illinois Chicago where I worked on statistical methods and matching algorithms for skeletal remains. I obtained my PhD in anthropology at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign under Lyle Konigsberg.

My primary research interests lie in machine learning applications in business and Responsible AI.
